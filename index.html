🔊 Sync with your music

burnTheWeb can pulse and flare in time with whatever you're listening to — turn on an audio source and the fire reacts to the beat.

Capture
Shares the sound of your screen or a browser tab — e.g. Spotify or YouTube. In the picker that opens, pick a screen/tab and tick “Share audio”.
Mic
Listens through your microphone instead — good for music playing in the room.

The audio is analysed only in your browser to find low/mid/high beats — nothing is recorded, stored or uploaded. Once it's on, arm any slider's L/M/H chips to make it jump on the beat.
